Best Things to do in Sri Lanka

HOT AIR BALLOONING
Hot air ballooning is a relatively new activity, Imagine yourself floating gracefully over the spectacular lands of Sri Lanka.
WHALE WATCHING
Whale watching is one of the most spectacular experiences to have in Sri Lanka. Just imagine these beautiful beings leap gracefully from the sea...
KITESURFING
Sri Lanka is a perfect place to get acquainted with kitesurfing and learn all the ropes. Start kiting in paradise and you won’t be able to stop!
BIRD WATCHING
Sri Lanka is literally a bird-watcher's treasure box. It is a bird watching paradise with a large number of birds that are endemic to Sri Lanka.
TRAIN TOUR - ELLA
The Kandy to Ella train ride is the most beautiful train ride in Sri Lanka, if not in the world! Board the train and experience breathtaking views of lush green tea plantations, mountains, viaducts, valleys, and dense jungle
SCUBA DIVING
One of the favourite recreational activities among tourists .There are several diving sites along Sri Lanka's coastline.
HIKING
Hiking is usually done up in the Hill country where mountains and water falls are found in abundance.
